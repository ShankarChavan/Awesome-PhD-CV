\begin{cvparagraph}

A robotics researcher focused on robust perception, state estimation for mobile robots, and lifelong mapping technologies essential for robotic navigation.
Studying algorithms that are robust to dynamic objects and outliers, with the goal of achieving generalization in real-world environments.
An enthusiast of open-source research, aiming to help others and contribute to the advancement of the robotics community.
Currently, a postdoctoral associate at the \href{https://web.mit.edu/sparklab/people.html}{SPARK Lab at MIT}.
Previously worked as a postdoctoral fellow at the \href{https://urobot.kaist.ac.kr/}{Urban Robotics Lab at KAIST}, Republic of Korea,
visiting scholar at \href{https://www.ipb.uni-bonn.de/}{StachnissLab, Univ. Bonn} in Germany, and research intern at \href{https://www.naverlabs.com/}{NAVER LABS}, Republic of Korea.
